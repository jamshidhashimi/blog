Here you can learn your facebook profile or your page ID; Edit the url and paste this to your browser address bar.

```
http://developers.facebook.com/tools/explorer/?method=GET&path=yourusername
```

To clear your website open graph cache, you can use this url

```
http://developers.facebook.com/tools/debug
```
