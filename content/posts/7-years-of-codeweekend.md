Hard to believe this was 7 years ago. Its uplifting to see this, and to know that I still feel the same excitement of running [CodeWeekend](https://codeweekend.net) with a group of amazing friends and colleagues as I did when we first got off the ground – we who collectively believed in the mission of educating the next generation of developers in [#Afghanistan](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/hashtag/?keywords=afghanistan&highlightedUpdateUrns=urn%3Ali%3Aactivity%3A6880726387574108160).

![](/images/posts/7-years-of-codeweekend/10498543_1525870004339853_8735497890837816467_o-1024x683.jpg)

December 25, 2014 – CodeWeekend Session

I am grateful to everyone who supported us, was part of our community and believed in our mission. I am grateful for the challenges and obstacles on the way as well, as they made us better.

To the next decade of doing this, to slow and steady and sustainable knowledge development, and most of all to the eventual and inevitable re-conquering of the agency and dignity of our people.

It will happen.
